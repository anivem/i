30-Day Muscle & Height Growth Plan

️

🌅 Morning Routine (Pre-Workout: 6:00 – 6:30 AM)
- 🥛 1 glass warm water + soaked: 5 almonds, 2 walnuts, 2 dates
- 🍌 1 banana OR 2 dates
- 🌻 1 tsp soaked flax + sunflower seed mix
- 🌿 1 tsp Mahabeera Ginjalu seeds (soaked)
- 🌿 Optional: 1 tsp ajwain in warm water

🍳 Post-Workout Breakfast (8:00 – 8:30 AM)
- 🥚 2 boiled eggs (whole)
- 🥔 1 boiled potato OR 1 small sweet potato
- 🥛 1 glass milk with turmeric + honey
- 🍌 1 fruit (banana, papaya, or mango)
- 🌱 Small bowl of boiled sprouts + spinach

⛅ Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 – 11:00 AM)
- 🥜 Mixed dry fruits: 2 dates, 5 almonds, 2 cashews, 3 walnuts, 1 tsp peanuts
- 🥛 Optional: 1 glass of milk
- 🌿 1 tsp soaked Sabja seeds
- 🥬 Salad of raw spinach + broccoli with lemon

⚽ Evening Snack (4:00 – 5:00 PM)
- 🥚 1 boiled egg OR fruit + dry fruits
- 🧂 1 bowl roasted chana + 1 tsp jaggery
- 🌻 1 tsp roasted flax/sunflower seed mix
- 🌿 Soanp (mouth freshener)

🌙 Before Bed (9:30 – 10:00 PM)
- 🥛 1 glass of warm milk + 1 tsp ghee
- 🌿 1 tsp soaked Mahabeera Ginjalu seeds
